What is Astrology?

Astrology is the art of understanding life’s rhythms and your soul’s unique path by reading the positions of the planets and stars at your time of birth. Through astrology, you can:

  • Discover your core strengths and challenges
  • Find meaning in repeating patterns and events
  • Gain insight into relationships and life decisions
  • Connect with your authentic self and cosmic purpose

Every session and reading is tailored to you, using your birth chart as a cosmic map to navigate life’s questions and opportunities.
